RAABTA~ Invisible threads make the strongest bonds "To care for someone who has cared for you is the highest honour. " Our elders be it our family members, our neighbour or a family friend, have always guided us with love throughout our life. This International Day of Families, Rotaract Club of RVCE brings to you "RAABTA". During this lockdown, teach them a modern life skill like video calling, using apps, YouTube, watching serials online or helping them with their personal chores, helping neighbour by bringing groceries etc. to make their everyday lives easier. Gift them all the happiness and care they deserve!
